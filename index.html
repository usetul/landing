About Usetul

Usetul is a trading app for modern investors who want to simplify decision-making. No need to study charts or indicators — we handpick strategies that are backtested and proven to be profitable over time. Just pick the one that fits your style, and receive alerts when it’s time to buy or sell.

Our Vision

We believe trading should be accessible, systematic, and stress-free. Even if you’re short on time or don’t know how to read charts, Usetul helps you trade more confidently — and more consistently.
